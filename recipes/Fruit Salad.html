Fruit Salad

Description:

A refreshing and healthy salad made with a variety of fresh fruits.

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 1 cup strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 tablespoon hone (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Wash and prepare all the fruits as needed, and place them in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Drizzle honey over the fruits if desired, and gently toss to coat.
  3. Transfer the fruit salad to a serving bowl or individual plates.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves for added freshness and presentation.
  5. Serve immediately, or chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
