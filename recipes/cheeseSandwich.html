Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

A comforting and easy-to-make grilled cheese sandwich, perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
  • Butter or margarine

Steps:

  1. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, onto the skillet.
  4. Add cheese slices on top of the bread.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown and the cheese starts to melt, then flip the sandwich.
  7. Cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is fully melted.
  8. Remove from the skillet, cut in half if desired, and serve hot.
