Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description:

A simple and flavorful Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving some pasta water.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic slices and red pepper flakes. Cook until garlic is golden and fragrant, but not burnt.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat with the garlic-infused oil.
  5. If the pasta seems dry, add a splash of reserved pasta water.
  6. Season with salt to taste and garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
  7. Serve hot, optionally with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
